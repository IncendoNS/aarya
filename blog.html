Reality check!

Ruchita Gacche.

Menstrual cups are coming more in trend now, which is lucrative because they are resusable and eco- friendly. But still a majority of the population considers it false advertising and continues to use sanitary pads. One of the main reasons in India being that people think that these amazing cups can't be used by virgins. Debunking this myth is very important to spread the benefits of it to more people. Many a times, virginity is thought of as something biologically connected to the hymen and whether it’s “intact.” Contrary to this belief, some are born without a hymen and activities like horseback riding, biking and gymnastics can stretch the hymen without you even knowing it. With that in mind, whether the hymen is intact or not has nothing to do with virginity. So stay away from this misconception and try a menstrual cup to save the environment and make your pockets heavier! But remember to be patient and give enough time before giving it up!

Change and acceptance!

As the quote by Leo Tolstoy says - " True life is lived when tiny changes occur." Change is inevitable, times change, dynamics change, and the people of the world change. And this change, for the betterment of the society, is possible due to the 'acceptance' of the generation to change. And for our record, we should also abide by the fact that a change is never wholely positive in the beginning, it takes time.

Any change, even for the good, is intimidating, at first. But what should follow is the mindset and the instincts to trust the growing technology and its powers to improve the quality of life.

There is a rapidly growing change in the field of menstrual hygiene - The menstrual cup. The menstrual cup is a boon for women wishing for low cost menstrual products, that last for longer durations without frequent changing, and for the icing on the cake - are environment friendly.

Honestly, did you find it easy to live your period with sanitary napkins when you first menstruated? You gave yourself a time of two/three cycles to get used to it right? You had no choice afterall.

Proper hygiene and sanitation of our intimate areas during the menstruation period is vital. We have a tendency to be rightfully, careful and protective of our intimate areas - and thus, the menstrual cup may seem like a change causing discomfort at first. All you have to do is trust the process and not give up.

This can be your best choice ever. Women record their feedbacks as "never going back to pads or tampons" because a menstrual cup can be as convenient as your routine. However, the myths surrounding periods and sanitary products are starting to bust. But when you read more about this menstrual product and listen to the women who are using it, you will know it better.

And this product, is now revolutionizing the menstrual hygiene field, with companies like Aarya producing safe and touch-free cups. This change is an utmost need in today's world to avoid anyone's day to be hindered by heavy and uncomfortable periods. So folks, lets join hands and make this perfect menstrual product a popular choice for safe and comfortable menstruation.
